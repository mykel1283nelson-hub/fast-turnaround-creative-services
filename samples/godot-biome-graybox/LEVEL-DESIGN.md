# Sunfall Hollow Level Design

## Player promise

A ten-minute biome slice that demonstrates the full explore, gather, rescue, upgrade, boss, and exit loop before production art is introduced.

## Critical path

`Village Hub -> Resource Loop -> Bridge -> Enemy Camp -> Boss Arena -> Portal`

The resource loop folds back toward the enemy camp, so optional-looking exploration still returns the player to the critical path. The boss arena and portal remain visible as distant landmarks from the eastern half of the biome.

## Encounter beats

| Beat | Purpose | Graybox evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Village hub | Establish safety and home base | Clustered huts, well, wide staging pad |
| Resource loop | Teach movement and route reading | Three pickups distributed around a return loop |
| Bridge | Compress the route and signal escalation | Narrow crossing between grove and camp |
| Enemy camp | First objective gate | Palisade, tents, captive, supply requirement |
| Boss arena | Culminating combat space | Circular floor, ten perimeter pillars, central boss silhouette |
| Exit portal | Confirm completion and point forward | Tall framed landmark beyond the arena |

## Production follow-up

- Replace labels with environmental signposting, lighting, and unique silhouettes.
- Test sightlines from the village and bridge after final terrain sculpting.
- Tune encounter density after movement and combat metrics exist.
- Add two optional discovery pockets without lengthening the critical path.
- Validate traversal time, resource readability, and boss-arena escape routes with player tests.
